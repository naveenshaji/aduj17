About NLUJA Assam

The National Law University and Judicial Academy, Assam (NLUJAA), situated in the heart of Brahmaputra valley, is a premier institution for imparting multi-disciplinary legal education.
The University, in keeping with its motto; In pursuit of excellence, has strived to produce world class legal scholars and practitioners since its very inception. NLUJAA boasts of an extremely erudite faculty to nurture and guide its students through its graduate, post-graduate, and doctoral programs.
Established by the ACT (XXV of 2009) of the Government of Assam, and recognized by UGC and Bar Council of India, in six years of its existence, NLUJAA has grown to become exemplary in rendering wholesome legal education and, maintaining superior professional standards in the legal community. With 13 centres dedicated to advanced research in various branches of Law and social sciences and, a mesmerizing campus on the banks of Brahmaputra, the institution vouches for a highly intellectually stimulating and jovial environment.

About ADUJ

It is with immense pleasure that we introduce the first instalment of ADUJ, an annual cultural and literary fest organized by the National Law University and Judicial Academy, Assam. ADUJ 2017 will be a three day cultural and literary extravaganza that would strive to unfurl the splendorous talents from all around the country. Beyond the fun and festivities, every year ADUJ will serve as a catalyst for raising awareness about a particular social issue.
This year, to bring this idea into praxis, ADUJ’17 has chosen to give its platform to voices raising awareness for child rights. Many of the events in the fest will especially be focused on a particular aspect of rights of children, providing the participants with an opportunity to contribute to the cause through their talents. ADUJ strives to serve an additional purpose of promoting and exhibiting the enormously rich and diverse cultural heritage of The North East.
